Fellowship Details – Six Steps to Success
Step Description
1 Become an IAGES Life Member (www.iages.in) or a member of collaborating associations
2 Apply for the Course and Conference Combo
3 Attend the full-day online session on Sunday, 19th October 2025
4 Reach Kanyakumari before 9 PM on 23rd October 2025 and attend sessions till 26th October 2025
5 Participate in MCQ written test and viva assessment
6 Mandatory attendance at academic sessions for your chosen fellowship course
Convocation Ceremony
On successful completion of all modules and assessments, fellowship certificates will be awarded during the convocation ceremony on 15th February 2026 at the IAGES ELSA Meeting in Mumbai.
